//01 — About

Who I am

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with 8 years of experience working at the overlap of brand, product, and motion. My background in fine arts gives me a way of seeing most designers don't — I'm drawn to texture, weight, and negative space.

I've shaped the visual identity of startups, rebranded legacy companies, and designed products used by millions. Every project starts with deep listening and ends with something that means something.

Based in San Francisco, currently open to select brand and product design collaborations.
